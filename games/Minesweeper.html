üî¢ Understanding Numbers

When you reveal a cell, it shows how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells:

  • 1 = 1 mine nearby
  • 2 = 2 mines nearby
  • 3 = 3 mines nearby
  • And so on up to 8
  • Blank cells have no adjacent mines

üö© Using Flags

Right-click cells you think contain mines to mark them with flags. This helps you keep track of dangerous cells and prevents accidental clicks.

üí° Tips & Strategy

  • Start by clicking corners or edges - they're safer
  • If a cell shows "1" and has only one unrevealed neighbor, that neighbor is a mine
  • Use the mine counter to track remaining mines
  • When you reveal a blank cell, it auto-reveals safe neighbors
  • Don't guess! Use logic to deduce mine locations

üèÜ Winning

Reveal all non-mine cells to win! You don't need to flag all mines - just avoid clicking them.
